Our Mission

The Flip Side is on a mission to do the impossible – give you nuanced arguments from both sides of the political aisle without your eyes glazing over.

We’re a one-stop shop for smart, concise summaries of political analysis from the two sides. Our goal is to become a news source for liberals, moderates, independents, conservatives, and even the apolitical.

It’s hard to convince liberals to watch Fox or conservatives to watch MSNBC. But, if everyone takes 5 minutes a day to read The Flip Side, we’ll have a starting point when talking to our friends and neighbors.
